On The Rise...

EsDeeKid

Esdeekid’s whole appeal comes from that underground energy; raw, unfiltered, and built from the ground up. He’s been carving out a niche with a sound that blends emotional storytelling and confidence, and his following keeps growing because people connect with the authenticity.

Personal #1 Favourite

Drake

Drake’s basically the blueprint for the modern mainstream rapper-singer. He can bounce between vulnerable R&B, high-energy club tracks, and sharp rap verses without dropping the momentum. His run over the last decade-plus is wild. He’s shaped the sound of popular hip hop more than almost anyone.

Big Fan Of...

J. Cole

Cole has that calm confidence, a storyteller who leans into introspection and perspective. He’s one of the few big artists who can go platinum with no features and still have people quoting his lines for years. His music hits because it feels real and rooted in something personal.

Gunna

Gunna has mastered that smooth, melodic trap lane. His flow just glides over beats, and he’s become one of the most consistent and influential voices in modern trap. Whether he’s doing something vibey or something heavier, he’s got a signature sound you can’t mistake.

Throwback Artist

50 Cent

50 came into the game like a force. His early run was pure dominance. Street stories, hooks you couldn’t get out of your head, and a presence that felt larger than life. Even now, his impact on hip hop, branding, and the business side of music is massive.
